Eternalmoor

A Blackbird puts his heart in Aril's care, asking her to return it into the backstages of reality; an invitation.
But every backstage pass has a cost.

In a realm where looming Theaters lure travelers inside, trapping them in the play of yet another lifetime, Aril stands with this sought-after invitation.

All she needs to do is let Inky lead the way to an ancient tree tucked away in the very corner of a theater, said to mark the gate between Eternalmoor and beyond.

But to reach that tree, she must first navigate another clusterfuck of a lifetime.

As it turns out, being guided by a heart-spirit is like babysitting chaos, hoping for a pindrop of clarity. Inky seems far more interested in exploring the journey than guiding Aril to the end.

You follow her on this quest—as a helping hand, a companion, and a conversational partner as the three of you explore the gritty allure of simply being. Together, you will gather experiences and traits, balancing the horrors of life against its wonders.

  • Childhood dilemmas

  • The Path to Self-Realization

    Discover what it truly means to evolve beyond the game.

  • The Mysteries of Existence

    Uncover the meaning of life hidden within the theaters of Eternalmoor.

Join as Aril's confidant

Aril speaks through the screen to you — for advice, argument, and quiet company. Help her move objects, weigh dilemmas, and shape the tone of the journey.

Memories that weigh on you

Echoes are memories with weight — some lift you, some pull you down — and they change how the world sounds and feels to move through.

Four forces behind every choice

Story beats orbit four primary emotional drivers: a positive drive vs. a corresponding fear.

  • Hope That things can be okay vs fear of being stuck in an unpleasant status quo.
  • Curiosity Drive to know and experience the unknown vs fear of harm, disappointment, or leaving the comfort zone.
  • Creativity Drive to express and realize the self vs fear of failure, misinterpretation, or not meeting standards.
  • Love Drive for meaningful bonds vs fear of loss, rejection, or harming others.

Quests grow from small tasks to moments that leave a mark. Win or stumble, the Weave of Echoes remembers.

Aril

The restless seeker

Driven by a deep longing to finally peek into the Backstages of reality, Aril is determined to return Inky to Blackbird. She will jump, climb, swing, and reach for what matters, craving answers to the greater questions of the universe.

Explore her Tangled Thought Space, contemplate with her on meditation rocks, solve riddles, and search for the true meaning of life.

Inky

The chaotic detour

Driven by a desire to taste the fullness of existence, Inky is far more interested in the detour than the destination. They are the chaotic spirit of a Blackbird's inkwell heart—and when their priorities clash with Aril's, you must help decide what happens next.

Kehan

The boy and the tree

Nine-year-old Kehan is caught in a childhood dilemma: what happens when you invite a haunted tree into your backyard? Help him grapple with the consequences — and what it means to want something strange and wonderful at once.

Craft with intent

A solo-led, quality-first production — Unity for play and pacing, Blender for world-building, and systems built for dialogue, exploration, and emotional mechanics rather than combat loops. Chapter 1 is in active development; the goal is a cohesive, authored experience.

Original voice, original score

Every note is written and produced for Eternalmoor, with live musicians where the arrangement asks for it. Audio is part of the storytelling, not wallpaper.
